Who Are We?

We are a Family of Black people, who have decided to come together,made up of different units, families and heritage to become a strong whole.

We come together to stand as a form of support, encouragement, empowerment and fun to every black person all over the world

We believe in creating memories and so we live and support each other to Live. Large. Create. Memories as they go.

Our History

We started 5 years ago, as just a large extended family related by blood. We sought to know every single member, no matter how far they are, and to what ends of the earth they Lived.

We sought to support each other, every way that we could, and hosted events to promote familiarity and ease stress.

But then, we made families, not related to us by blood, and decided to expand this, to all black families around the world.

We promote and Capture y(our) moment and save them on our platform.
